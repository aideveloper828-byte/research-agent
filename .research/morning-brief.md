## Headlines

- **AI adoption crossed 58%** among PM companies — AppFolio Realm-X users saving 10+ hrs/wk; the question is no longer whether to adopt AI, but which tools deliver real ROI
- **NOI under structural siege**: Insurance costs up 55% since 2021 (~$800/unit); maintenance materials still 30% above pre-pandemic; 93% of PM companies saw expenses rise last year
- **Multifamily stabilizing, not booming**: National rent growth at 0.2% YoY; vacancy 4.4%; Sunbelt markets running 200bps higher — those operators are hurting and most receptive to retention tools
- **Retention gap is real and fixable**: Industry targets 63% renewal rates, averages 58%; 40% of at-risk renters say better maintenance would make them stay — managers are blaming "life changes," residents are blaming poor service
- **PropTech M&A surged to 163 deals in 11 months of 2025** (vs. 134 all of 2024) — consolidation accelerating; Opiniion's integration partners may be acquirers or acquisition targets

## What Changed Recently

- **AppFolio** is now the only PM platform rated for "Autonomous Task Execution" (80%) on G2 — Realm-X is ahead of competitors on agentic capability
- **Buildium's 2026 Industry Report** (just released) shows 75% of PM companies report increased fraud — a growing secondary buying driver for PM software
- **California AB 325** restricts algorithm-based rent pricing (effective 2026) — directly impacts RealPage/YieldStar-style tools; opens compliance positioning opportunities
- **Construction pipeline** is down 53% from 2023 peak — sets up 2027–2028 market tightening and potential rent growth recovery
- **BTR (build-to-rent)** segment growing faster than traditional multifamily due to lower regulatory exposure — underserved by most PM point solutions

## Action Items (Top 3)

1. **Build CFO-facing ROI model** — Quantify churn cost vs. Opiniion subscription cost for PM company finance buyers. Key metric: 1% retention improvement on a 1,000-unit portfolio = $30K–$50K in avoided turnover costs. Finance leaders are the new buying center.

2. **Audit integration depth with top partners** — Map actual data-flow depth (not just logo presence) with AppFolio, Buildium, Rent Manager, MRI, Yardi. Flag which partners are building native feedback features that compete with Opiniion's core. Do this before next M&A cycle claims a key partner.

3. **Target Sunbelt markets in pipeline** — Austin, Phoenix, Denver, and Southeast operators face highest vacancy pressure (6%+) and are most motivated for retention tools. Run targeted outbound campaign Q2–Q3.
