# Source Quality Ratings

## Tier 1 — High Signal (Consistently valuable)
- **Gartner press releases** — Best for adoption rates and trend data. Multiple surveys per year.
- **L.E.K. Consulting Office of CFO Survey** — Excellent for quantified before/after metrics
- **Kyriba CFO surveys** — Good for strategic AI adoption data among larger companies
- **Commercial Observer** — Strong PropTech funding and deal coverage

## Tier 2 — Good Signal
- **CFO Dive** — Solid synthesis of Gartner/Forrester data with practitioner context
- **CFO.com** — Good for ROI and skeptic perspectives
- **Numeric blog** — Useful for mid-market close tool comparisons (note: vendor bias)
- **Forrester blogs** — Good for AR/AP automation frameworks
- **PwC Real Estate Trends** — Valuable for PropTech strategic direction

## Tier 3 — Use With Caution
- **Vendor comparison pages** (Ramp vs Brex, FloQast vs BlackLine) — Useful data but inherent bias
- **G2 award announcements** — Directionally useful, marketing-driven

## Not Yet Rated
- SaaStr content, Bessemer Cloud Index, KeyBanc SaaS Survey, Pavilion/RevGenius communities
- Need to search these in future sessions
